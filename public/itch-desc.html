About

In the near-distant future, Phase Connect has been bought out by Kuro Company, where you are joining as a new Talent Manager. Hire and train your Talents to meet the company's ever-increasing Viewer Goals and reach 1M Subscribers!

How to Play

  • Before you stream, check out the Shop
    • Here you can buy Talents. Buying a Talent adds them to your Bench (see the Stream window).
    • Each Talent has a unique Talent Ability. Mouse over them to see what it does.
    • If you want to buy more Talents or see different ones, click the Restock button to reroll the current set of Talents (for a price)
    • Pay attention to Inflation, which increases multiplicatively each day!
  • Click Stream to open the KBS window
    • This window controls which Talents are active during a Stream and what Abilities they will use.
    • You start with one random Talent.
    • If you have two of the same Talent (check the Shop), you can merge them to gain XP and level up.
      • Leveling up gives Max Stamina and an Ability Slot!
      • Talents start with no Ability Slots
    •  Talents in the Stream Preview will be active during Stream. Talents on the Bench will not be active.
    • Pay attention to the Viewer Goal. You must reach at least this many Viewers during Stream or you will be fired.
    • Abilities
      • After each stream, you will be given a choice of 3 Abilities. Mouse over them to find out what they do.
      • Cooldown: The time it takes for a talent to use the Ability
      • Once chosen, abilities are added to your Inventory.
      • Drag an ability to a Talent's Ability Slot, and they will use it during stream.
    • When you're ready, click Start Streaming to start the Stream!
  • During Stream
    • Pay attention to your numbers!
      • Views
        • The amount of people who have clicked on the stream, but are not necessarily viewing it right now
      • Views/sec
        • The number of Views you are gaining per second.
        • This starts at 10 each stream.
      • Viewer Retention (VR)
        • Percentage of Views that become Viewers
      • Viewers
        • The amount of people currently watching the stream.
      • Viewer Goal
        • The number of Viewers that management wants you to reach
        • When you meet the Viewer Goal, you are given a $5 Performance Bonus.
        • Meeting the Viewer Goal multiple times over grants you multiple Performance Bonuses.
      • Subscription Rate (SR)
        • Percentage of new Viewers that become Subscribers
      • Subscribers
        • Subscribers influence the effectiveness of certain Abilities
        • Subscribers are carried over between Streams
      • Membership Rate (MR)
        • Percentage of new Subscribers that become Members
      • Members
        • Members give you $1.00 per Member, per Stream
    • Talents
      • During a Stream, your talents use the abilities they have to make numbers go up.
      • The Stream's length is limited by your Talents' Stamina. Stamina drains at a rate of -1 per second, and the drain increases over time.
